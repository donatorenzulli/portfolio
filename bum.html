BUM | La Marittima

BUM was a unique project in which 3D photogrammetry and film footage were combined to create stunning live visuals for a music event. We used Blender and TouchDesigner to bring their vision to life. The final result was a visually stunning live performance that combined 3D photogrammetry, film footage, and dynamic visual effects to create an immersive and unforgettable experience for the audience. The BUM project demonstrated the power of combining different mediums and software programs to create truly unique and innovative visual experiences.
