Golden Goose - HAUS

The headquarter becomes a theater where Golden Goose shares its values through an innovative user journey entirely designed by Dotdotdot and the brand, to provide a unique experience. At the heart of the HAUS architecture is an immersive space with a mix of physical and digital elements that embodies the essence of the brand. The experience concept draws inspiration from nearby Venice, echoing the brand's spirit and legacy. It begins with a deep immersion in the Venetian canals, followed by a flight above the city toward a starlit sky.
