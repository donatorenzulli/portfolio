about

hello there! I am Donato Renzulli, I am 25 years old and I currently live in Milan. I have a bachelor's and master's degree in communication design from PoliMi. I was lucky enough to live two Erasmus experiences, the first at IADE (Lisbon) and the second at Aalto univesity (Helsinki). During these years I have become very passionate about using technology to create visual languages that can translate the complexity of the world around us into something cool to see!

One of the projects I am particularly proud of is Generact, an association we founded with friends from my city (Foggia, Puglia) with which we try to propose active citizenship by redeveloping abandoned and underused spaces through cultural events. Check it out!
