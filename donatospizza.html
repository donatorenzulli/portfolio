Donato's Pizza

Donatoâ€™s Pizza is a short film of 3D animation made with Blender. The goal was to represent the passions of the members of the group through the three protagonists of the story: music (Donato), dance (Caterina) and computer science (Manuel). A unique pizzeria is represented in which, through ironic episodes and break-dance moves, a pizza is prepared for an important delivery order. All 3d models and animations were made by the members of the group. A soundtrack has also been created with AbletonLive, a fundamental element for the final video editing.
