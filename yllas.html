Ylläs

The concept takes inspiration from the feeling I had the first time I tried skiing. Ylläs is an interactive installation in which the viewer stands on a little steep platform in front of an abstract organic fluid tunnel. When tilting down, the impression will be the one falling faster into the tunnel. When moving left and right, the tunnel will follow in real-time the body movement. In order to get the experience more immersive, a wind sound will increase volume while the viewer is going faster and will move to left/right according to the position of the tunnel.

Visual and interaction: Touchdesigner, Mediapipe
Sound: MAX-MSP
