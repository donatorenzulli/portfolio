Sometimes it happens

"Sometimes it happens" is my contribution for the exhibition "Drawing with closed eyes" at Aalto University (April 2023). It's a series of postcards made with p5.js, all of them generated with random function. It's a way to combine digital and analog media. It's a reflection about randomness in life.
