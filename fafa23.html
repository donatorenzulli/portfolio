FAFA FEST 2023

Design and realisation of the visual identity for the FAFA FEST 2023 (Foggia, Italy). The festival was created by Generact, the association founded in our hometown to reactivate underused public spaces. For the 2023 edition, I created an interactive installation using the visual language of the festival's visual identity and a webcam with which the public could interact.
